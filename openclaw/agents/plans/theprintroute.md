## Site description

3D printing knowledge hub for makers and hobbyists. Focus: practical guides, print-in-place designs, filament selection, printer reviews, and functional prints. Target audience: hobbyist makers, engineers who print functional parts, beginners getting started. Differentiator: hands-on, no-fluff approach with real print settings and outcomes.

## Pillars (5 content themes)

1. **Printer reviews & comparisons** – Bambu Lab, Prusa, Ender, Voron. Hands-on takes with real print results, not spec sheets. Keywords: "best 3D printer 2025", "Bambu Lab A1 review", "Prusa MK4 vs Bambu".
2. **Filament guides** – PLA, PETG, ASA, TPU, nylon. When to use what, brand comparisons, print settings. Keywords: "best PLA filament 2025", "PETG print settings", "ASA vs ABS 3D printing".
3. **Print-in-place & functional designs** – hinges, gears, snap-fits, living hinges. Settings and model sources. Keywords: "print in place 3D printing", "functional 3D prints", "best print in place models".
4. **Tutorials & troubleshooting** – stringing, warping, layer adhesion, supports, bed adhesion. Fix-it guides. Keywords: "3D print stringing fix", "how to reduce warping", "3D printing troubleshooting guide".
5. **Software & slicing** – OrcaSlicer, PrusaSlicer, Bambu Studio tips. Profile sharing, calibration guides. Keywords: "OrcaSlicer tutorial", "best slicer settings 3D printing", "Bambu Studio tips".
