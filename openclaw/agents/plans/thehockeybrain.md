## Site description

Hockey analytics consulting and intelligence platform. Target audience: coaches, GMs, player agents, analytics staff at club and national-team level. Differentiator: combines traditional scouting with modern data (Corsi, xG, WAR, player-tracking) and AI-assisted analysis.

## Pillars (5 content themes)

1. **Advanced hockey analytics fundamentals** – Corsi, Fenwick, xG, Expected Goals, PDO, zone entry/exit, RAPM, WAR. Educational explainers for staff who want to go from "what does Corsi mean" to "how do we use it in line-matching".
2. **AI & machine learning in hockey** – predictive models for player performance, injury risk, draft rankings, video analysis automation, GPT-assisted scouting reports. Very high search growth 2025-2026.
3. **Player development & tracking** – modern tracking data (TPE, skating speed, acceleration zones), load management, development curves, comparing junior/AHL/NHL trajectories.
4. **Coaching & team strategy** – power-play and penalty-kill systems, deployment analytics, line combination optimization, game-script awareness.
5. **Business of hockey analytics** – how clubs build analytics departments, ROI of data infrastructure, salary cap modelling, trade value analytics.
