## Site description

Decentralized technology and Web3 infrastructure platform. Focus: atomic ownership models, cross-chain protocols, DeFi architecture, and the intersection of decentralized systems with AI. Target audience: developers, protocol designers, DeFi power users, Web3 investors.

## Pillars (5 content themes)

1. **Atomic ownership & token models** – fractional ownership, atomic swaps, token-gated access, ownership primitives, property rights on-chain. Core differentiator of the brand.
2. **Cross-chain interoperability** – bridge protocols, LayerZero, Chainlink CCIP, IBC, Wormhole, chain abstraction. One of the hottest developer topics 2025-2026.
3. **Decentralized AI infrastructure** – on-chain AI compute (Bittensor, Gensyn, Akash), verifiable inference, federated learning, AI x crypto convergence. Fastest-growing search category.
4. **DeFi protocol architecture** – AMMs, lending protocols, restaking (EigenLayer), liquid staking, modular blockchains (Celestia, Avail). Technical depth for builders.
5. **Web3 developer tooling** – smart contract auditing, testing frameworks (Foundry, Hardhat), indexers (The Graph), account abstraction (ERC-4337), toolchain comparisons.
