## Site description

Accessible hockey analytics for coaches, scouts, and players. More approachable than thehockeybrain – focus on "how to use analytics in practice" rather than deep mathematical dives. Target audience: junior and senior coaches, player agents, scouts, team managers who want to start using data. Differentiator: practical, jargon-light, action-oriented guides.

## Pillars (5 content themes)

1. **Analytics for coaches** – how to use data in line-matching, power-play design, deployment decisions. No PhD required. Keywords: "hockey analytics for coaches", "using data in hockey coaching", "hockey stats for coaches".
2. **Player development metrics** – how to track and interpret development over time. For agents, parents, and coaches. Keywords: "hockey player development analytics", "how to evaluate junior hockey players", "player tracking hockey".
3. **Scouting with data** – using stats to find undervalued players, compare leagues, identify draft targets. Keywords: "hockey scouting analytics", "data-driven hockey scouting", "hockey draft analytics".
4. **Explainers & glossary** – Corsi, xG, WAR, PDO explained simply for non-analysts. Internal link target. Keywords: "hockey stats explained", "what does Corsi mean", "hockey analytics glossary".
5. **Tools & software** – accessible tools: InStat, Coach's Eye, Sportlogiq lite, free resources. Keywords: "best hockey analytics software", "free hockey stats tools", "hockey data for small clubs".
